The Story Behind Swapifhy

Founder Story

Anwesha founded Swapifhy after noticing how many talented students had skills they were proud of, but no real space to exchange them in a way that felt natural, creative, and genuinely exciting.

What started as a simple idea slowly turned into a bigger question: what if learning did not have to feel transactional? What if instead of paying for every lesson, people could teach what they knew and learn what they loved in return?

That thought became the early version of Swapifhy. A place where a guitarist could connect with an editor, a designer could team up with a writer, and students could build cool things together just by trading what they were already good at.

Swapifhy was built to make learning feel more human, more collaborative, and a lot more alive.
